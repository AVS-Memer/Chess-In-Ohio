Notation

Notation is written as: Piece (2 UPPERCASE LETTERS), Square From (1 or 2 lowercase letters and a number, only used if there is confusion), Square To (1 or 2 lowercase letters and a number). An example would be NTag46

Special Moves

  • Castle to the right: O-O
  • Castle to the left: O-O-O
  • Castle vertically: O-O-O-O
  • Sacrifice: NPS
  • Reproduction: R
  • Amazonian reproduction: AZ-R
  • Skip: 0
  • Originality: OR
  • Resigner: %(coordinates)
  • 6 on dice: D6
