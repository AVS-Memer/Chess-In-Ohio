There are many moves you can do other than moving pieces, which would still take up a move.

Special moves

Castle

Move the king 2 spaces towards a rook. move the rook to the space between where the king was and the king is. The king and rook cannot have moved before this.

En Passant

If a pawn moves 2 spaces, the opponent's pawn can still capture the pawn by going to the space that the pawn skipped.

Sacrifice

Take 9 pawns and replace them with a chancellor. You can still do your move.

Resignation draw

If the resigner kills the king or last prince and they would normally lose, the game would become a draw.

Reproduction

The king and queen must be adjacent to each other for 2 moves, and you can declare when you want the process to begin. This counts as a move. The queen is unable to move for the next 10 moves, and there must be an open space next to the queen to make a prince, otherwise the prince is aborted.

Other choices for moves

Originality

The opponent can only move one of the 6 original pieces from normal chess.

Rage

Throw the board at your opponent, and fight them. If you win, the game is a draw. If you lose, you lose the game.

Dice

Roll a six sided die.

1

Your opponent wins.

2

You use the originality move

3

Have a normal move.

4

You tell your opponent which piece is your resigner.

5

Reroll

6

Your opponent loses their most powerful piece.

Wordle

You think of a 5 letter word. The opponent gets 6 chances to guess it. For every guess, If your opponent guesses it, they get a niftic, and you decide where you put it. If your opponent fails to guess it, you get an amazon, and your opponent decides where to put it, but they may not take it next move.

Spawn ducktif

You can place a ducktif somewhere. There have to be at least 2 squares without a ducktif on the board for you to put one.

L

Put an L in front of your opponent as they do their next move.

Minecraft

Put a Minecraft block somewhere to block your opponent's view.

Pay to win

Give a dollar to your opponent for a bishop that can be placed on any square.

Be loud

See who talks louder. The louder person will get a pawn-gouch.

Pawn combine

Flip a coin. If heads, take any 8 pawns and turn them into a zeta

Punch

Flip a coin. If heads, punch your opponent in the face. If tails, they get to punch you in the face.

Trash talk

Trash talk to your opponent. They will receive a pawn-gouch.

Llama bet

You say "llama betcha" and your opponent has to say the number of llamas you have in the next second. If they don't say it, all of your llamas obtain the ability to jump other pieces like a horse (they're still known as llamas)
